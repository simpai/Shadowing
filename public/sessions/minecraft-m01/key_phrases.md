# Useful Phrases & Patterns for Minecraft English (30-Day Plan)

These patterns are key syntax structures for middle school students, demonstrated with Minecraft examples.

## 1. Advice & Suggestions (제안 및 조언)
*   **You should [verb]...** (~하는 게 좋아)
    *   *You should make a bed before night comes.*
*   **How about [verb-ing]...?** (~하는 건 어때?)
    *   *How about building a treehouse here?*
*   **Make sure to [verb]...** (꼭 ~하도록 해)
    *   *Make sure to bring enough torches.*
*   **Don't forget to [verb]...** (~하는 걸 잊지 마)
    *   *Don't forget to eat when you are hungry.*

## 2. Necessity & Ability (필요 및 능력)
*   **I need to [verb]...** (난 ~해야 해)
    *   *I need to find some iron for a shield.*
*   **We have to [verb]...** (우린 ~해야만 해)
    *   *We have to run! A zombie is coming!*
*   **I'm able to [verb]... / I can [verb]...** (난 ~할 수 있어)
    *   *I'm able to craft a diamond sword now.*

## 3. Describing Processes (과정 설명)
*   **First, [verb]..., then [verb]...** (먼저 ~하고, 그 다음에 ~해)
    *   *First, chop the wood, then make planks.*
*   **It takes [time] to [verb]...** (~하는 데 [시간]이 걸려)
    *   *It takes a long time to find diamonds.*
*   **Keep [verb-ing] until...** (~할 때까지 계속 ~해)
    *   *Keep digging until you see bedrock.*

## 4. Experiences & Plans (경험 및 계획)
*   **I have never [past participle]...** (난 ~해본 적이 없어)
    *   *I have never seen the Ender Dragon.*
*   **I used to [verb]...** (난 (예전에) ~하곤 했어)
    *   *I used to live in a cave, but now I have a house.*
*   **I am planning to [verb]...** (난 ~할 계획이야)
    *   *I am planning to build a farm tomorrow.*
*   **I'm going to [verb]...** (난 ~할 거야)
    *   *I'm going to explore the Nether.*

## 5. Feelings & Opinions (감정 및 의견)
*   **It looks [adjective].** (~게 보여)
    *   *This cave looks dangerous.*
*   **I'm worried about [noun/verb-ing].** (~가 걱정돼)
    *   *I'm worried about falling into lava.*
*   **I think (that) [sentence].** (내 생각엔 ~인 것 같아)
    *   *I think we are lost.*
*   **It's [adjective] to [verb].** (~하는 것은 ~해 / 가주어 it)
    *   *It's fun to play with friends.*
    *   *It's hard to find emeralds.*

## 6. Location & Direction (위치 및 방향)
*   **There is a [noun] [preposition]...** (~에 ~가 있어)
    *   *There is a village across the river.*
*   **It's closely/far from...** (~에서 가까워/멀어)
    *   *Our base is far from the spawn point.*
*   **Go straight and turn [left/right].** (쭉 가다가 ~로 꺾어)
    *   *Go straight and dig down.*

## 7. Collaborative Play (협동)
*   **Let's [verb]...** (~하자)
    *   *Let's sleep now so we can skip the night.*
*   **Can you give me [noun]?** (~ 좀 줄래?)
    *   *Can you give me some food?*
*   **Watch out!** (조심해!)
    *   *Watch out! There's a creeper behind you!*
