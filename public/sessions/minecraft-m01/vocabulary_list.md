# Middle School Vocabulary & Idioms for Minecraft English (30-Day Plan)

This list contains vocabulary and idioms suitable for Korean middle school students. The words are selected to be useful in both the game (Minecraft) and daily life.

## Verbs (Action & Interaction)
1. **gather** - 모으다 (Gathering wood/resources)
2. **explore** - 탐험하다 (Exploring the map)
3. **craft** - 만들다, 공예하다 (Crafting tools)
4. **survive** - 살아남다 (Survival mode)
5. **protect** - 보호하다 (Protecting the village)
6. **attack** - 공격하다 (Attacking mobs)
7. **defend** - 방어하다 (Defending against zombies)
8. **trade** - 거래하다 (Trading with villagers)
9. **discover** - 발견하다 (Discovering a temple)
10. **destroy** - 파괴하다 (Destroying blocks)
11. **build** - 짓다, 건설하다 (Building a house)
12. **equip** - 장착하다 (Equipping armor)
13. **repair** - 수리하다 (Repairing tools)
14. **enchant** - 마법을 걸다 (Enchanting weapons)
15. **explore** - 탐험하다
16. **avoid** - 피하다 (Avoiding creepers)
17. **collect** - 수집하다
18. **share** - 공유하다, 나누다
19. **suggest** - 제안하다
20. **decide** - 결정하다

## Idioms & Phrasal Verbs (Real-life & Game)
1. **watch out for** - ~을 조심하다 (Watch out for the Creeper!)
2. **run out of** - ~이 다 떨어지다 (We ran out of food.)
3. **give up** - 포기하다 (Don't give up mining.)
4. **look for** - ~을 찾다 (Looking for diamonds.)
5. **figure out** - 알아내다, 이해하다 (Figure out how to build.)
6. **depend on** - ~에 의존하다, 달려있다 (It depends on the biome.)
7. **get ready** - 준비하다 (Get ready for the night.)
8. **take care of** - ~을 돌보다, 처리하다 (Take care of the farm.)
9. **make sure** - 확인하다, 확실히 하다 (Make sure you have a torch.)
10. **get lost** - 길을 잃다 (Used to get lost in caves.)
11. **pay attention to** - ~에 주의를 기울이다
12. **turn into** - ~로 변하다 (Turn wood into planks.)
13. **keep away** - 멀리하다, 접근하지 못하게 하다
14. **deal with** - 다루다, 처리하다 (Dealing with spiders.)
15. **lighting up** - 밝히다 (Lighting up the cave)
